Trader Frequently Asked Questions

Your Questions Answered

1. How do I request a pickup?

+

Go to the “Pickup Request” section, select material types from the catalog, enter estimated weight and pickup location, then submit.

2. What types of materials can I recycle?

+

Only materials listed in the official catalog (e.g., Cotton, Denim, Polyester). You’ll see the list when making a request in the app.

3. Do I need to sort the items?

+

Yes. Please sort your items by material type as shown in the catalog for faster processing and fair payment.

4. How much will I be paid?

+

Each material has a price per kilogram, visible in the catalog. Payment is made after weighing and verification.

5. How long does it take for pickup to happen?

+

Pickups are scheduled within 24–72 hours after your request is confirmed.

6. Is there a cost for pickup?

+

No, pickups are completely free for verified traders.

7. Where can I see my payment status?

+

Check the “Payments” tab in your dashboard to view payment updates.

8. What if the recycling company doesn’t show up?

+

Use the Help section or in-app contact options to follow up or reschedule.

9. Can I edit or cancel a pickup request?

+

Yes, but only within 24 hours of submitting the request.

10. How does the points system work?

+

Each successful paid pickup earns you loyalty points. These accumulate automatically in your profile.

11. What happens when I reach 500 points?

+

You unlock a chance to spin a reward wheel. You must spin within 7 days before the points reset to zero.

1. How do I claim my reward after spinning?

+

You must physically collect your reward at the recycling company. Details will be shared in your app after you win.
